Who is Jose Rizal?

Jos√© Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda (born June 19, 1861, Calamba, Philippines and died December 30, 1896, Manila) is one of the most revered figures in Philippine history. He was a multifaceted intellectual and a political activist, best known for his political writings that inspired the Philippine revolution and ultimately led to his execution by the Spanish colonizers. Rizal was also a physician who trained in ophthalmology under 2 prominent European ophthalmologists, Louis de Wecker and Otto Becker.

During 1880s he published his renowed novel, Noli me Tangere and was followed by a sequel, El FIlibusterismo which both spoke of the evils of Spanish rule in the Philippines.
